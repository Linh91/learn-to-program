99 Bottles of Beer on the wall

start_num = 5
present_num = start_num
while present_num > 2
  puts present_num.to_s + ' bottles of beer on the wall, ' +
    present_num.to_s + ' bottles of beer\!'
  present_num = present_num - 1

  puts 'Take one down, pass it around, ' +
    present_num.to_s + ' bottles of beer on the wall\!'
  end
  puts 2 bottles of beer on the wall, 2 bottles of beer!
  puts Take one down, pass it around, 1 bottles of beer on the wall!
  puts 1 bottles of beer on the wall, 1 bottles of beer!
  puts Take one down, pass it around, no more bottles of beer on the wall!
